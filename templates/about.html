About Me


Hello There,


I am Dayanna, originally from Brazil and I have been in the United States for 4 years. I am currently working as a nanny with a great passion for my job as a childcare worker. That being said, besides being Available for babysitting your loved one, I am also available to help you out being your new Personal Assistant! If you would like to know more information about it, feel free to contact me or check my experiences and references on LINKEDIN and URBANSITTER . I hope I can help you to bring organization and free time for your busy schedule as a parent.
